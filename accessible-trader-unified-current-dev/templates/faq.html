Frequently Asked Questions

What is Accessible Trader?
A fully accessible trading platform built for blind and visually impaired traders, offering keyboard navigation, speech feedback, and sonified charts.
Which markets do you support?
Crypto, stocks, indices, commodities, and forex - each in one consolidated interface.
How do I place a trade?
Trading entry will be available from the dashboard once you connect an exchange API key. Stay tuned for our Trade module!
Is my data secure?
All sensitive information is stored encrypted, and we follow best practices for authentication, authorization, and secure connections.
