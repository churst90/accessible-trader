Welcome to Accessible Trader

Your gateway to a fully accessible and inclusive trading experience, designed for traders who are blind or visually impaired.

Why Accessible Trader?

Trading can be challenging for everyone, but it's especially daunting for those who rely on non-visual tools. Accessible Trader bridges the gap, empowering you to explore, analyze, and execute trades confidently.

Core Features

  • Multi-Sensory Charts: Sound, tactile feedback, speech and visual styling.
  • Custom Indicators: Add, remove, save & recall indicator setups.
  • All Markets: Crypto, stocks, indices, commodities, forex.
  • Audio & Speech Feedback: Fully configurable keyboard-driven interactions.
  • Advanced Tools: Bots, algorithms, pattern-detection and alerts.
  • Comparisons: Pair & compare assets beyond USD.
